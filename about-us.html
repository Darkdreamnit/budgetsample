Who We Are

Event Budget Sample was founded by Kevin Pommells who was frustrated by the lack of accurate budgeting tools that were available on the market. I have set out to create a solution that will help professionals plan better events with financial accuracy.

Our Mission

The mission I set is to help event professionals with accurate budgeting tools. My aim is to provide insights that remove the guesswork from financial planning which in turn allows professionals to focus on creating accurate and memorable experiences.

Our Vision

Within the next couple of years, I would like for Event Budgeting Sample to become the worldwide standard for event budgeting. I want this tool to help transform how the budgeting industry approach financial planning through data-driven insights and innovative technology.
